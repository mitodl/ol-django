### Fixed

- Harden the Keycloak SCIM client: reconcile already-synced users by their
  stored `scim_external_id`/`global_id` instead of re-matching on email,
  isolate bulk-create and search batch failures so one bad chunk no longer
  aborts the rest of a sync run, re-queue (with backoff) just the users
  that failed to sync instead of losing them until the next full sweep,
  fail a search batch outright instead of guessing when it contains a
  duplicate join key (e.g. two local users sharing an email), and stop a
  SCIM replace with a missing `externalId` from blanking out a user's
  `global_id`.
